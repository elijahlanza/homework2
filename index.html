About Me

It was in this department that I spent a majority of my time focusing on my writing skills. I would play a major role in helping to build the Wells Fargo resolution team. After 2 years I left the resolutions team and went to work within the Wells Fargo private bank. I would go on the work with some of the richest people in Utah and accross the country. Finally, this experience would lead me to the coding boot camp preparing for a new career as a developer
